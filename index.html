About Celeste

Real Estate Is Personal

I had such a great experience purchasing my first home in 1998 that in 2002, after leaving active duty in the Army, I became a realtor. I feel a connection with people every day — I help them buy and sell their most precious asset: home.

From Washington, DC to San Juan, Puerto Rico, and now the beautiful Carolina coast, I bring multifaceted experience to every negotiation.

What drives me? The look on my clients' faces when I've made a dream into a reality.
